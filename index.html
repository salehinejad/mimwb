About Us

The MIM lab is focused on medical imaging processing and big data analytics. Our expertise bridges the gap between data warehouses, modern algorithms, high performance computing and better outcomes for patients, better and more cost-effective workflows, and product development. We have an active relationship with tech transfer at St. Michael's Hospital, MaRS and a variety of commercial interests.

Our Team
